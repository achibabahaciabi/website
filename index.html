Cubo: An unorthodox 3D box game-like design tool.

  • Simple to use and navigate in its dome space.
  • 20x20x20 cube volume.
  • Paint cubes with hex RGB color wheel.
  • 3D Animation with desired speed.
  • Save/load your project to retrieve whenever you want.
  • Save as .obj file.
  • Additional instructions are provided in its menu.
